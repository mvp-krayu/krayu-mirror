# Portfolio Intelligence

<p class="thesis">Compare execution stability across programs.</p>

Portfolio Intelligence allows leadership to compare **Execution Stability Index (ESI)** scores across programs and workstreams.

## What is ESI?

ESI measures the structural stability of a technology program across multiple dimensions.

### Key dimensions

- Schedule Stability
- Cost Stability
- Risk Acceleration Gradient
- Delivery Predictability
- Flow Compression

## ESI Score Bands

| ESI | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 85–100 | Stable |
| 70–84 | Early instability |
| 55–69 | Structural stress |
| <55 | Critical risk |
