# Program Intelligence Applied

How the [Program Intelligence](/program-intelligence/) discipline becomes operational — and what it produces.

---

## From Evidence to Structural Intelligence

Program Intelligence delivers a structural view of program health from validated delivery evidence.

The analytical process does not begin from dashboards or management summaries. It begins from execution evidence drawn directly from the engineering environment and processed through a governed derivation chain that maintains traceability from source to output.

Delivery evidence is drawn from existing engineering systems without instrumentation. No additional tooling is required. Evidence is collected at the source, and every output produced downstream remains traceable to it.

The output is traceable executive intelligence, not descriptive reporting. Each signal is derived under governed rules. Each output is bounded to what the evidence supports — not extrapolated from estimates, not filled in where evidence is absent.

---

## The Signal Infrastructure

Signäl is the execution signal infrastructure through which Program Intelligence is delivered.

Signäl operationalizes the [Signal Infrastructure](/signal-infrastructure/) capability — the governed system that receives execution evidence from engineering environments and produces the structural signals that Program Intelligence depends on. Where activity data shows what is occurring, the signal infrastructure derives what it means for the program as a structured system.

The result is not a rephrasing of engineering metrics. It is a governed structural account of program health, derived from evidence and structured for decision-making.

Learn more about how [PiOS](/pios/) governs the derivation chain that produces these signals.

---

## The Engagement

LENS Assessment is the bounded engagement that produces this intelligence as a single governed artifact.

The engagement is time-bounded, requires minimal client effort at intake, and delivers one artifact: a governed executive intelligence view of the program's structural state. It is accompanied by a review session that converts the artifact into a decision-relevant discussion.

There are no ongoing reporting requirements. No instrumentation changes. No extended system access. The engagement begins with evidence intake and ends with a governed artifact that leadership can rely on when steering the program.
