# Program Intelligence Research

<p class="thesis">An examination of the structural visibility problem in technology organizations.</p>

Technology organizations produce enormous operational data. Yet executive leadership cannot reliably interpret program execution.

## The Visibility Problem

The challenge is interpretive: raw engineering telemetry does not speak the language of executive decision-making.

## Defining Program Intelligence

| Discipline | Focus | Audience |
|---|---|---|
| Engineering Observability | System health | Engineers |
| Business Intelligence | Business KPIs | Business leaders |
| **Program Intelligence** | Program structure and delivery risk | Executive leadership |
